World War I (1914–1918)

A global conflict mainly in Europe caused by political tensions, alliances, and the assassination of Archduke Franz Ferdinand.

World War II (1939–1945)

The largest war in history involving countries worldwide. It ended after Germany and Japan were defeated.

Cold War (1947–1991)

A political and military rivalry between the United States and the Soviet Union.
