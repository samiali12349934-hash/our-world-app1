🌍 The Story of Our World

The world is the planet we live on. It is called Earth. Earth formed about 4.5 billion years ago.

🌋 Structure of Earth

  • Crust – The surface we live on
  • Mantle – Under the crust
  • Core – Very hot center

🌎 Continents

  • Africa
  • Asia
  • Europe
  • North America
  • South America
  • Australia
  • Antarctica

Earth has oceans, mountains, forests, deserts and many living things.
