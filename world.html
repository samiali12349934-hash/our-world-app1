The Beginning

The Earth formed about 4.5 billion years ago. Over millions of years, oceans formed, continents shifted, and life began in the seas.

Human Civilization

Humans created ancient civilizations like Egypt, Rome, and China. They built cities, invented writing, and developed cultures.

The Modern World

Today the world is connected through technology, trade, and communication. But we still face challenges like climate change and global conflicts.
